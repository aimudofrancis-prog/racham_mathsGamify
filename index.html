⚔️ Math Battle Game ⚔️

Player HP: 100 Enemy HP: 100 Score: 0

How to Play

  • Use arrow keys to move the yellow box (Player).
  • Collide with the blue box to answer math questions.
  • Solve correctly to earn points.
  • Get 8 correct answers to level up.
  • Reach Level 10 to win the game!
  • Score, level, and HP are displayed above the canvas.
